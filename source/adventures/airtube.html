Simply Drag & Drop

AirTube lets you stream web videos from many of your favorite websites to your Apple TV.

Just drag the clip you want to play from your browser onto the AirTube icon and you're set!

We've tested AirTube to work with YouTube, Vimeo, TED, Funny or Die and quite a few others.

Remote Control the Video

You can seek back and forth in the clip using AirTube on your Mac.

Add Support for More Sites

Because we rely on the excellent quvi project it's pretty easy to add support for other websites. All you need is a path to the MP4 video.

We supply the default set of Quvi scripts with AirTube, but you can drag lua scripts on the AirTube dock icon to add it to the collection.
