Easy network access

Need to share some files, or a screen? Want to manage a printer via its web interface? Maybe even check out that website your colleague is working on. Coucou makes it super easy to perform these tasks.

It also gives you a nice overview of which devices (and their owners) are currently present.

Coucou uses Apple's Bonjour protocol to detect services on your network.

Works great for developers

If you're a web developer you'll like the built-in Bonjour broadcaster. Just select the service and port and everyone around you will be able to easily select it from the list.
