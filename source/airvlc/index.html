AirVLC uses VLC

No need for a sub-par video player. AirVLC merely controls the awesome video player that you already know and love. Make sure you download VLC before getting started.

Get rid of the audio delay

AirPlay causes a delay in the audio, which makes videos unwatchable. AirVLC helps to overcome that by suggesting the right delay preset.

Works great with Mountain Lion

Mountain Lion supports streaming to a single AirPlay speaker out of the box. Do you have multiple speakers or are you on Snow Leopard or Lion? Try Porthole!

Works with local files and YouTube

This means that you can watch any .avi, .mkv, .mp4 you have lying around, but it also works with YouTube links. Drag a link from your browser onto AirVLC and you're done!

Simply Drag & Drop

Select files on your Mac and drag them all over to AirVLC. If you select multiple files, VLC will automatically put them in a playlist.

The same goes for online videos. Just drag the thumbnail or link onto AirVLC and it'll handle the rest.

Multiple AirPlay Speakers or OS X 10.6/10.7

Do you have multiple AirPlay speakers? Experience your movie throughout your entire house, playing in sync via every AirPlay ouput!

Porthole will also allow you to AirPlay even if you're on OS X Snow Leopard or Lion.
