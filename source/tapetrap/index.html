Tapetrap Keep track of your web video feeds.

Watching online videos is about following the creative people who make them, getting notified about new content and keeping track of what you've seen.

Not just for YouTube or Vimeo, but any site with a feed.

Want to be the first to try Tapetrap for Mac? Sign up for the mailing list to stay informed about updates and help testing.

A new app for Macs, created with grog by Danger Cove.
