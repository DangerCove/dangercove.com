What does 'beta' mean, exactly?

I've been working on Tapetrap for a couple of months now and I'm curious to hear what you think of it. The purpose of the beta is to gather feedback and explore new features (and find horrible bugs, of course).

The beta is free to use and fully functional. The final version of Tapetrap will will be a paid app, priced between $ 5 and $ 25.
